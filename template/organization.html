Institut Teknologi Bandung

Institut Teknologi Bandung (abbreviated as ITB) is a state, coeducational research university located in Bandung, Indonesia. Established in 1920, ITB is the oldest and the first technology-oriented university in Indonesia.

2018 - now

Unit Budaya Lampung ITB

Unit Budaya Lampung ITB (abbreviated as Ubala ITB) is a student club in Institut Teknologi Bandung for students who are interested in preservation and development of Lampungnese culture. It is established based on kinship and culture.

2018 - now

Keluarga Mahasiswa Islam ITB

Keluarga Mahasiswa Islam ITB (abbreviated as Gamais ITB) is a student club which cope Moslem students on whole Institut Teknologi Bandung. It become a coaching-based professional institution with services and networks that inspire the movement of the entire campus community.

2019 - now

Himpunan Mahasiswa Informatika ITB

Himpunan Mahasiswa Informatika ITB (abbreviated as HMIF ITB) is a student club which cope Informatics Engineering and Information Technology and System students on whole Institut Teknologi Bandung. It is established to fulfill the needs of Informatics Engineering and Information Technology and System students, such as academic, cocurricular, profession, and welfare.
